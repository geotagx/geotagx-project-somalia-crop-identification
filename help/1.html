This is a simple filtering question to remove any photos that do not have crops in them and therefore are not relevant to the analysis.

Click "Yes" if you see crop plants in the photo

If you are not sure what you are looking at, look for some of the following signs:

  • Machinery, tools, irrigation, and other items associated with agriculture
  • Plants are in rows
  • Fences, hedgerows or other marking the boundaries of a field
  • Homogeneity in the field - all the plants are the same species, the same height, and/or look the same age

You should also click "Yes" if the crop appears dead as these can still give lots of information about the environmental conditions in the area

Click "No" if you are looking at a natural environment or an agricultural field without crops.

Natural environments can be recognised by the following:

  • A variety of species growing in the same area
  • Different layers in the vegetation that may include an understorey (such as grasses), a midstorey (such as smaller trees and shrubs), and an overstorey (the taller trees)
